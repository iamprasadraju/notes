##  **Operators**

### Arithmetic Operators

|Operator|Description|
|---|---|
|`+`|Addition|
|`-`|Subtraction|
|`*`|Multiplication|
|`/`|Division|
|`%`|Modulo (remainder)|

---

### Compound Assignment Operators

|Expression|Equivalent To|
|---|---|
|`y += x;`|`y = y + x;`|
|`x -= 5;`|`x = x - 5;`|
|`x /= y;`|`x = x / y;`|
|`price *= units + 1;`|`price = price * (units + 1);`|

**Other compound operators:**

- `+=`, `-=`, `*=`, `/=`, `%=`
    
- `>>=`, `<<=`, `&=`, `^=`, `|=`
