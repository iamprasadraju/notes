## Key Principles

> [!note]
> Concepts are the building blocks of understanding. Each concept note captures a single idea that can be connected to others.

### Atomicity

Each note should capture one idea, clearly and completely.

### Connectivity

Notes become powerful when linked. The web of connections creates emergent understanding.

### Iteration

Notes should evolve over time. Revisit and refine as your understanding deepens.
